## HP Elite x3

An interesting phone to say the least. One of the rarest production Windows Phone devices. Bought in February, 2024 for 250 PLN.  
Came with the box, dock and all the original documentation. Used mainly as a playground for Continuum and UWP.

### Fun facts

- I first heard about this phone in 2018, when I had a Lumia 530. Only remembered about it after seeing the same video in 2024.
- I never planned to get the barcode scanner, as it came out to be 200 PLN + shipping, but it was too rare to skip
- The phone is only 6 inches, but due to it having a 16:9 screen it's super wide
- The Bang & Olufsen speakers definitely do deserve their brand

### Barcode scanner

Bought in March, 2024 for 200 PLN, had to be imported from Germany. I thought it would work like a USB input device – boy was I wrong.  
After many days of searching I finally came across a Microsoft SDK Sample, which included a BarcodeScanner project.  
Intrigued, I got out my Elite x3 and tried it out. To my surprise, it actually worked and I finally had a way to use the barcode scanner!
