## Nokia Lumia 1020 64GB

A special version of the famous Nokia Lumia 1020 mobile phone released exclusively in the UK, on Vodafone and O~2~.  
Bought in June, 2024 for 30 PLN on Allegro. One of, if not the rarest production Windows Phones in existence. In perfect condition.

## Fun facts

- I originally thought someone else had bought it until it arrived and my dad told me he bought it right after seeing it
- This specific example is really wanted in the community because of it's storage and the fact I have HERE offline maps on it.
- I use is as my main digital camera due to it's extensive camera controls and the fact it can shoot DNG's
- Many backgrounds in the [Live Store](https://store.live.net.co) were shot on this exact phone
